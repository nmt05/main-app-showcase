Problem And Audience

Understanding the gap we identified and the people we built this for.

The Problem

Most brain-training apps focus on memory, math, or language puzzles. Very few train divided attention through simultaneous bimanual input. Existing alternatives like Lumosity or Brain It On require only one hand at a time, leaving dual-hand coordination skills largely untrained.

Target Users

  • Young students (high school and university) looking for brain exercises
  • Brain training enthusiasts who want a focused dual-hand challenge
  • Casual mobile gamers during commute or short breaks
  • Anyone interested in improving hand-eye coordination and reaction speed

Value Proposition

GameTwoShapes delivers a simple yet effective brain exercise that trains hand coordination and focused attention in under a minute per session. Draw two shapes at once, beat your high score, and build bimanual dexterity through a gameplay loop designed for fast learning and progressive challenge.

What Makes It Unique

Unlike any competitor in the market, GameTwoShapes requires drawing two different shapes simultaneously with both hands. This mechanic creates a rare bimanual coordination challenge that no mainstream brain-training app currently offers.

How The Loop Works

A clear three-step rhythm keeps the game easy to start and difficult to dominate.

01

Read Targets

Two shape targets appear on separate sides of the screen. You have limited time to process both at once.

02

Draw Both

Use multi-touch to draw the correct shape on each side. The system recognizes circles, squares, triangles, and diagonal lines.

03

Survive Pressure

Each correct turn boosts your score and shortens the time window, forcing sharper focus and faster hands.

Key MVP Features

Each block below maps directly to the mobile game experience and what makes the project unique.

Dual-Hand Control

Split-screen interface with independent left and right canvases. Each hand draws a different shape using multi-touch input.

Progressive Difficulty

Round speed ramps up after every correct answer, turning simple controls into a serious concentration test.

Score And High Score

Local high-score persistence encourages replay. Instant restart flow keeps players chasing their personal best.

Shape Recognition

Built-in gesture recognition validates drawn shapes against templates for circles, squares, triangles, and diagonal lines.
